FAQ

  • How do I unlock new levels?

    Complete levels sequentially to unlock further puzzles.

  • Can I skip a level?

    Currently, levels must be completed in order to progress.

  • Are there any in-app purchases?

    The game offers in-app purchases for hints and level packs.

  • What do I do if a puzzle seems unsolvable?

    Use the hint function or reset the puzzle to try different approaches.

  • How can I save my game progress?

    Progress is automatically saved in your device's memory.

  • Is there a multiplayer mode?

    This version of Line Puzzle is single-player only.
