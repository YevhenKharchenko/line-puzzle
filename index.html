About the Game

"Line Puzzle" invites players to a mentally stimulating environment where the objective is to solve complex puzzles by manipulating various lines on a grid. The game offers a unique blend of relaxation and mental challenge, encouraging players to stretch, bend, and connect lines to form specific shapes or patterns. With hundreds of levels, each increasing in difficulty, players encounter an array of obstacles and unique scenarios that require logical thinking and creativity. The interface is minimalistic and user-friendly, designed to keep the focus on puzzle-solving without unnecessary distractions.
